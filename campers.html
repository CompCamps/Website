MSI CompCamps

Annual Summer Camps

Every year, CompCamps provides one-week camps for students aged 13-17. The camps provide a fun environment to explore technology with a group of like-minded peers. Campers are able to pick one or more areas to focus on for a project that they will demo at the end of the week.
